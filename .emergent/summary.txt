**Code Architecture**


**Key Technical Concepts**
- **Scored Readiness State:** A backend service that evaluates multiple system metrics (mapping completeness, hard failures, drift) and aggregates them into a single, prioritized readiness score to guide operator actions.
- **Idempotent 1-Click Actions:** Backend API endpoints designed to be safe-by-default. They ensure that repeated calls do not cause unintended side effects and include pre-checks and post-verification.
- **Automated Rollout Gates:** A backend service that codifies the success criteria for moving between production rollout phases, making the promotion process automatic and data-driven rather than manual.
- **State Snapshot WebSocket Streaming:** A pattern where a background worker periodically computes an aggregated state snapshot of the system and pushes it over WebSockets, providing a real-time feel to the UI without overwhelming it with a high frequency of individual events.
